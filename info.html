What is Ocean Raiders

Ocean Raiders is a dynamic, youth-led global organization dedicated to the protection of whales and the preservation of marine ecosystems. Driven by passion, innovation, and a deep respect for the oceans, we leverage the power of science, technology, and activism to create lasting, tangible change for our planetâ€™s marine life.

Why Whales?

Whales are the guardians of the seas. They regulate marine ecosystems, help capture carbon, and ensure a healthy balance in our oceans. Without them, marine life suffers and climate change worsens. Protecting whales means protecting the planet.

Global Reach

With volunteers, researchers, and activists across continents, Ocean Raiders builds bridges between communities. We collaborate with schools, NGOs, and innovators to inspire action and implement real-world solutions for marine challenges.
