Whale Species

Learn about the giants of the ocean we strive to protect.

Blue Whale

The largest animal ever known to exist on Earth, growing up to 100 feet long. Despite their size, they feed mainly on tiny krill and play a crucial role in ocean carbon storage.

Humpback Whale

Known for their acrobatic breaches and melodic songs, humpbacks migrate thousands of miles each year between feeding and breeding grounds.

Orca (Killer Whale)

Apex predators with strong family bonds, orcas are found in every ocean. Their intelligence and adaptability make them one of the most remarkable species.
